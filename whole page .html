About Our School

At New Heavens New Earth School, we are dedicated to providing a holistic educational experience that nurtures every child's intellectual, emotional, social, and spiritual growth. Our commitment is to create a safe, stimulating, and inclusive environment where young learners can thrive.

We believe in fostering curiosity, critical thinking, and a love for learning. Our experienced educators use innovative teaching methods to bring out the best in each student, preparing them not just for academic success, but for life.

Our Mission

To inspire and empower children to achieve their full potential through quality education and strong values.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for excellence in nurturing future leaders and compassionate citizens.

Our Academic Excellence

At New Heavens New Earth, our academic approach is designed to inspire a lifelong love for learning. We follow a comprehensive curriculum that meets national standards while incorporating innovative teaching methodologies to engage every student.

Our dedicated teachers foster an environment where students are encouraged to ask questions, explore new ideas, and develop critical thinking skills. We focus on foundational subjects while also providing opportunities for creative expression and personal development.

Key Academic Areas:

  • **Literacy:** Reading, Writing, and Communication Skills
  • **Numeracy:** Mathematics and Problem-Solving
  • **Science:** Discovery and Exploration of the World
  • **Social Studies:** History, Geography, and Civics
  • **Creative Arts:** Music, Art, and Drama
  • **Physical Education:** Health and Movement

Admissions

We welcome prospective students and families to join the New Heavens New Earth family. Our admission process is designed to be straightforward and supportive.

How to Apply:

  1. Fill out our online application form.
  2. Submit the filled form along with required documents to the school office.
  3. Schedule an entrance exam and/or interview for your child.
  4. Receive admission offer and complete enrollment formalities.
